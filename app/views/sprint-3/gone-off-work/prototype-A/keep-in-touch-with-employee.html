You should keep in touch with your employee while they are absent from work

Your employee has been off work for 6 weeks. It's best to get in touch in the first week they're off work, and then every two weeks until they are ready to return.

Staying in regular contact with your employee is the best way to help them come back to work when they are ready.

Keeping in contact is a good chance to:

  • check on the wellbeing of your employee
  • see if they need any support
  • talk about any relevant updates or changes happening at work
